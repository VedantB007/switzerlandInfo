 A BRIEF ABOUT SWITZERLAND 

  1.) Switzerland is a small mountainous country located in central Europe.

  2.) This landlocked country is about the size of New Jersey and is between France and Italy.

  3.) It is also bordered by Austria, Germany, and Liechtenstein.

  4.) Most of the population lives in the plateau which is between the high Alps in the south and the Jura mountains in the north.

  5.) The mountainous area in the south is sparsely populated.

  6.) Switzerland is one of the world’s wealthiest countries.

  7.) The Swiss are well known for their watches and clocks.

  8.) There is not a single official language in Switzerland.

  9.) People speak one of several languages, including Swiss German, French, and Italian.

  10.) The country is made up of 26 cantons or states, which form the confederation.
